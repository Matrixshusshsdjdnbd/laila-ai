LAILA AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

LAILA AI ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered smart assistant created by Bathie Sarr. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using LAILA AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password (encrypted) when you create an account.
  • Chat Data: Messages you send to the AI assistant, including text, images, and voice recordings.
  • User Preferences: Language preferences, voice settings, and app settings you configure.
  • Smart Memory: Information you voluntarily share during conversations (name, goals, preferences) that LAILA remembers to personalize your experience. You can view, disable, or delete this at any time in Settings.
  • Payment Information: Transaction records for premium subscriptions (payment details are processed securely by third-party providers — Wave, Orange Money — and we do not store your financial credentials).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered chat, translation, image analysis, and content generation services.
  • To personalize your experience using your preferences and smart memory.
  • To process premium subscription payments.
  • To improve our AI responses and app performance.
  • To send important service updates (not marketing).

4. AI Processing

Your messages and images are processed by OpenAI's GPT-4o model to generate responses. Voice recordings are processed by OpenAI's Whisper model for speech-to-text conversion. These services process your data to provide responses and do not use it for training.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers.
  • Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 encryption and are never stored in plain text.
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers.
  • Session tokens expire after 7 days for security.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with:

  • OpenAI: To process AI requests (chat, image analysis, voice transcription, text-to-speech).
  • Payment providers: Wave, Orange Money, or other payment processors for subscription payments.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • View memory: See what LAILA remembers about you in Settings → Memory.
  • Clear memory: Delete all stored memories at any time.
  • Disable memory: Turn off smart memory entirely in Settings.
  • Delete conversations: Delete individual or all chat history.
  • Delete account: Contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

8. Children's Privacy

LAILA AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Chat history is stored until you delete it. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
