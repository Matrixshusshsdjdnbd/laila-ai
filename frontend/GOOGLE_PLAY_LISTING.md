## Full Description (4000 chars max)

LAILA AI is a powerful, free AI assistant built specifically for people in Africa.

Created by Bathie Sarr, LAILA AI helps you with work, study, business, and daily life — all from your phone.

🤖 SMART AI CHAT
Ask LAILA anything and get clear, practical answers. LAILA speaks like a smart friend — not a robot. Get help with any topic: work, education, health, technology, finance, and more.

🌍 MULTILINGUAL
Write in your language, get answers in your language:
• Wolof
• Français
• English
• Italiano
LAILA automatically detects your language and responds naturally.

💼 WORK & CAREER
• Create professional CVs instantly
• Find job opportunities (LinkedIn, Jobberman, Expat-Dakar, Indeed)
• Prepare for interviews
• Write professional emails and messages

💰 BUSINESS IDEAS
• Practical business ideas starting from 5000 FCFA
• WhatsApp commerce strategies
• Social media marketing tips
• Mobile money business (Wave, Orange Money, M-Pesa)

📚 STUDY HELP
• Step-by-step homework explanations
• Math, science, reading, writing — any subject
• Like having a patient personal tutor

📸 IMAGE ANALYSIS & GENERATION
• Take a photo and ask LAILA to explain it
• Translate text in images
• Generate images from text descriptions
• Help with documents and forms

🎤 VOICE FEATURES
• Speak instead of typing with voice-to-text
• Listen to LAILA's responses with text-to-speech
• Choose from 3 voice styles
• Perfect for hands-free use

🧠 SMART MEMORY
LAILA remembers your preferences, name, and goals to give you personalized help. You control what LAILA remembers — view, clear, or disable memory anytime.

🌐 TRANSLATION
Translate between Wolof, French, English, and Italian with AI-powered accuracy.

⭐ PREMIUM FEATURES
Free users get 20 messages/day. Upgrade to Premium for:
• Unlimited messages
• Full voice features
• Image analysis & generation
• Advanced AI assistants
• Pay with Wave or Orange Money

👥 INVITE FRIENDS
Share LAILA with friends and earn free Premium days!

LAILA AI is optimized for low-end Android devices — fast, lightweight, and reliable even with slow internet connections.

Built with love for Africa. 🌍
